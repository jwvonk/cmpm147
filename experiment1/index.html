Experiment 01 - Hello Glitch

Description

I want the audience to read these patch notes and laugh about a) how absurd the implied pre-existing "bug" that the patch-note describes is, and b) how strange it would be to be the person whose job it is to "fix" the bug. Then, I want them to look at some actual absurd patch notes (like the ones that this generator was based on) and realize that there actually ARE bugs this absurd in real video games, and there ARE actual people whose jobs it is to fix these ridiculous bugs--and just revel in the absurdity of it all.

Technical

A grammar is defined with simple replacement tokens. A replacer() function picks a random replacement for any token that appears in the text.

Reflection

I worked alone on this project. The hardest part was getting started; blank canvases are always difficult for me. I was really proud of myself when I thought of moving the content of the template string into individual fillers. I am very pleased with the final result and how absurd the results are while still being completely reasonable patch notes.
